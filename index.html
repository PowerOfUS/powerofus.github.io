Power of Us

The Power of Us Suicide Prevention Network (SPN) Inc. is a group created within the Brisbane South-Asian community to promote the importance of mental health and suicide prevention to the greater Brisbane Transcultural Community.

We are kindly supported by Wesley Mission and the Vendanta Centre.
